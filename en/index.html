FAQ

The questions that matter before you download.

The page should make the decision easy. If you still need something answered, support is one email away.

Do I need an account?

No. Home-Check works without sign-up. You can install the app, add devices, and start planning maintenance immediately.

What is included in the free version?

The free version includes the full maintenance workflow with reminders, templates, search and filters for up to 3 devices.

What does Pro unlock?

Pro removes ads and unlocks unlimited devices, which makes it a better fit for larger households or multiple locations.

How do reminders work?

Home-Check schedules local notifications 3 days before the due date, on the due date, and again if the task becomes overdue.

Where is my maintenance data stored?

Your maintenance data is managed locally on the iPhone. Analytics, ads and privacy details are documented transparently in the privacy policy.
